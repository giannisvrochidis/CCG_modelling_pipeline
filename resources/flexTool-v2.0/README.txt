IRENA FlexTool is an energy system planning tool aimed for analysing the flexibility of power systems operating with variable power generation.
The tool is documented at the IRENA website: https://www.irena.org/energytransition/Energy-System-Models-and-Data/IRENA-FlexTool 

The tool has been developed by Juha Kiviluoma (VTT), Tomi J. Lindroos (VTT) and Simo Rissanen (formerly of VTT) for and with IRENA.

For any question or comment contact: Flextool@irena.org
